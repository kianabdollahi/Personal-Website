About me

Hello!, my name is Kian Abdollahi, a 21 year old student here at BCIT. Born in Ontario, my family quickly decided that our lives would be better off in a small french town near the swiss border. My dad being from Iran, did not enjoy Ontario winters, and rightfully so.

My parents,brother, and I lived there for about 7 years until we decided to move once again, this time to Switzerland, and finally when I was 11 back to Canada. I have moved quite a lot in my life, and I am incredibly grateful for the experiences that my parents have given me. Even though moving was never easy, it has made me a more adaptable and knowledgeable person.
